# Better Giving

> Nonprofit fundraising and fund management platform — by a nonprofit, for nonprofits. Accept donations via card, crypto, stocks, DAF, and more. Grow funds with savings and investment options. Free for nonprofits.

Better Giving is a 501(c)(3) public charity (EIN 87-3758939) that provides free donation processing, embeddable fundraising forms, fund management (high-yield savings and investment portfolio), and fiscal sponsorship for international NGOs. 195+ countries, 100+ cryptocurrencies, zero platform fees.
